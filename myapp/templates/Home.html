Woreda Residence Management System

This is our WRMS project.

Welcome

We came up with the idea of this project after considering the current state of the Ethiopian woreda system. The woreda system in our country is in disarray. It is not well managed, it is difficult for getting many service by current lazy system .our project woreda residence management system is empowered to handle many services like land administration, Giving ID cards, marriage certificates and birth certificates, policing services, collection of land rents/taxes, collection of house rents, land ownership authorization and transfer of properties, etc. As a result, society of Addis Ababa woreda has benefited from such an arrangement as most services are brought closer to the community; there by avoiding lengthy bureaucratic procedures delays that makes the number of days large to perform a job during the old system.
